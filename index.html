Robots

Have you ever thought about how kids get to school? Sometimes they could get a ride from their parents, they could take a train or bus, or maybe even walk. Have you ever thought about people who live in remote locations? Sometimes, it's really hard for them to get around. We want this problem to be solved.
Say hello to the telepresence robot. The idea behing the telepresence robot is to give users the ability to project their presence to a remote location through a robot. We feel like this is an important part in education, espicially for kids who can't get to school all the time.

Kids with disabilities

We think this will be very beneficial for kids with disabilties. Some disabilities leave kids unable to walk or do anything basically. Home schooling is an option but what if they want the education from a professional? What if they want the school experience? It isn't fair that these kids aren't able to get a chance to experience this. For these kids, our goal is to raise enough money to pay for half of their experience. If they really need the money, we will give only the ones who need it the most the money
